about

QualityProducts is one of newly launched brand in the FMCG consumable space. QualityProducts has a range of products including Aluminum Kitchen Foil and Aluminium Containers which are 100% recycleable.

We are fully committed to the safety and hygiene of our consumers and ensure that all our products maintain the “QualityProducts standards” which include highest quality products with food grading, bacteria resistance and delivery as per mentioned specifications.

To further add value to the lives of our consumers we plan to launch other daily consumables in the near future, including tissue papers.

We have state of the art manufacturing facility located near Mumbai which is certified as per global standards. Like our products, our manufacturing process and set up is also environment friendly and falls under green category.
